NCC UNIT HR BN 11

Why is NCC important for students?

The NCC provides exposure to the cadets in a wide range of activities., with a distinct emphasis on Social Services, Discipline and Adventure Training. The NCC is open to all regular students of schools and colleges on a voluntary basis.

THE NSS UNIT

Why is NSS important for students?

NSS provides students with opportunities to develop various skills, such as leadership, communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. Engaging in different types of community projects exposes students to real-world situations that require them to think creatively and work collaboratively.
