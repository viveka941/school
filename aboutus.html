History And Discription of the School:

A.R.E.D. SR. SEC. SCHOOL was established in 1997 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in DOHKA HARIYA , BADHRA block of CHARKHI DADRI district of HARYANA. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English and Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
